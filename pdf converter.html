Cancellation & Refund Policy:
1) Cancellation policy
ReserveYourRide will process the ticket cancellation request from You as per the cancellation/refund policy of the bus operator.
The cancellation fee and the period taken to process the refund will differ for each bus operator. Please refer to the bus operator's cancellation policy.
2) How does the customer cancel a ticket?
Please call on 080-3941 2345 for all cancellation related queries.
Our customer support team will help you process the request as per the cancellation policy of the bus operator.
Click here to cancel your booking.
3) Refund Policy
i) Customer will be entitled to a 100% refund of ticket fare if the cancellation of the journey is attributable to either ReserveYourRide or the bus operator.
Customer will not be entitled to refund for cancellation of tickets for any other reasons.
ii) In the event of dishonour of ticket by the bus operator, a full refund of the ticket fare would be processed within seventy two (72) hours. The refund would be credited to the customer within seven (7) working days of the dishonour of ticket.
